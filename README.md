# 🍗 Al Baik Restaurant Management & Ordering App

A modern restaurant ordering and management application inspired by Al Baik, built using Stitch and Google AI Studio. This project is designed to streamline restaurant operations by combining customer ordering, waiter assistance, and powerful administrative tools into a single platform.

## 🚀 Features

### 🛒 Customer Ordering System
- Easy food ordering interface.
- Browse menu items and place orders quickly.
- Home delivery option available.
- Simple and user-friendly design.

### 👨‍🍳 Waiter Ordering System
- Waiters can take orders directly through the app.
- Orders instantly appear in the kitchen/order management section.
- Reduces manual errors and improves service speed.
- Eliminates the need for handwritten order slips.

### 📋 Order Management
- View all incoming orders in real time.
- Separate sections for:
  - Pending Orders
  - Preparing Orders
  - Served Orders
- Track the status of every order efficiently.

### 📦 Delivery Management
- Support for home delivery orders.
- Manage delivery requests from the admin dashboard.
- Keep track of customer delivery orders.

### 📊 Sales Analytics
- Automatically calculates total sales based on completed orders.
- Provides restaurant owners with quick business insights.
- Eliminates manual sales calculations.

### 🔐 Admin Dashboard
The administrator portal is the core of the application and includes:
- Order management
- Sales tracking
- Delivery management
- Restaurant performance monitoring
- Real-time operational control
